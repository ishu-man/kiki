But what even is FSRS?

FSRS stands for Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler (see Research) Think of it as a smart tutor for your memory. Unlike older methods that use fixed guesses, FSRS is a modern algorithm that analyzes your personal review history to understand how your memory works. It models your unique "forgetting curve" for each piece of information, predicting the perfect moment to show you a flashcard againâ€”right before you're about to forget it. The result? You achieve maximum long-term retention with significantly fewer reviews, making your learning sessions incredibly efficient. It's science-backed learning, tailored just for you.
